FAQs

How do I access AI Co-Founder and Co-Founder Actions? +
Access is available via your account dashboard navigate to the "Co-Founder" section and enable Actions from the settings. Contact support if you need assistance.
What kind of questions can I ask AI Co-Founder? +
You can ask strategic, operational, and technical questions related to your project — from planning and prioritisation to example code and marketing ideas.
What can Co-Founder Actions do? +
Actions can generate content, create tasks, suggest code changes, and help automate common workflows. Capabilities depend on your subscription level.
Does AI Co-Founder provide legal or tax advice? +
No — AI Co-Founder provides general guidance and templates but does not replace professional legal, tax, or financial advice. Consult a qualified professional for those matters.
